# Example Research Proposal — Image Classification with Attention

## Title
AttentionNet: Improving Image Classification with Multi-Head Self-Attention Layers

## Abstract
We propose AttentionNet, a convolutional neural network augmented with multi-head
self-attention layers for image classification on CIFAR-10 and ImageNet-100. Our
approach integrates attention mechanisms after each residual block to capture
long-range spatial dependencies. We demonstrate improved accuracy over baseline
ResNet models while maintaining comparable computational cost.

## Experiments

### Experiment 1: Baseline Comparison
Compare AttentionNet against ResNet-50, ViT-Small, and DeiT-Small on CIFAR-10.
Report top-1 accuracy, training time, and parameter count.
Results directory: results/baseline_comparison/

### Experiment 2: Ablation Study
Evaluate the contribution of each attention component:
- No attention (baseline ResNet)
- Single-head attention
- Multi-head attention (4 heads)
- Multi-head attention (8 heads)
Results directory: results/ablation_study/

### Experiment 3: Training Convergence
Plot training and validation loss/accuracy curves over 200 epochs for each model
variant. Show learning rate schedule overlay.
Results directory: results/training_curves/

### Experiment 4: Attention Visualization
Generate attention heatmaps for selected test images showing which spatial
regions the model attends to at different layers.
Results directory: results/attention_maps/
