1. Overall aesthetic: Clean, publication-style bar chart typical of ML/biomedical papers; minimal decoration, emphasis on quantitative comparison with bold axes and large typography.

2. Color palette: Muted categorical palette with solid fills — approx. light gray #D3D3D3 (Prime‑2.1), pale yellow #F2EFA5 (NetMHCpan), light pink #F7D6DA (MHCnuggets), lavender #D9BFD8 (MHCflurry), tan/orange #D9A97C (DeepNeo), very light green #E3F5E1 (BigMHC‑EL), light green #B7E1B0 (BigMHC‑IM), medium green #8FD08A (BigMHC retrained), strong blue #3B73B9 (ImmunoStruct, primary highlight).

3. Typography: Helvetica throughout; default font size 24 pt for ticks/legend; y‑axis labels enlarged to ~32 pt; no titles shown on subplots.

4. Layout: Single-row multi-panel layout (1×4 overall, three metric panels plus legend area); very wide aspect ratio (~28×6 inches); generous horizontal spacing; no x‑tick labels to reduce clutter.

5. Data encoding: Vertical bar charts; uniform bar width; solid color fills; black error bars showing standard deviation with capsize ≈5 points; no hatching or markers.

6. Axis styling: Left and bottom spines only (top/right removed); thick spines (~3 pt linewidth); no gridlines; y‑axis ranges tightly set per panel; ticks outward with default length.

7. Legend: Placed to the far right outside the plotting panels; vertical list; no visible frame; font size ~24 pt; single column with 9 entries.
