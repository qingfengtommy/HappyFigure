Overall Aesthetic
The style is "Top-Tier Genomics Publication" (e.g., Nature Genetics or Nature Biotechnology). It is characterized by a high-density multi-panel layout, a clean white background, and a "flat" vector-art style. There is a strong emphasis on comparing specific methods using a consistent color code across different chart types (bar, scatter, and genomic tracks).
Color Palette
Use a specific categorical trio for the main method comparisons (Panels c, e, f, i, j, k):
Method A (Blue - e.g., AlphaGenome): Muted Medium Blue (#628CC5)
Method B (Lime/Yellow - e.g., Borzoi): Muted Chartreuse/Lime (#DCE775 or #D4E157)
Method C (Teal - e.g., Enformer): Muted Teal/Turquoise (#66C2A5)
Baseline/Control: Dashed Black line or Dark Grey (#404040).
Secondary Palettes:
Schematic (Panel a): Pastel Blue (#D6EAF8) and Pastel Red (#FADBD8) for input blocks.
Genomic Tracks (Panel b): Cyan/Light Blue (#5DADE2) for REF, Muted Red (#EC7063) for ALT. Highlight region in Bright Gold (#F1C40F).
Stacked Bars (Panel h): Salmon (#EC7063), Muted Purple (#C39BD3), Blue (#628CC5), Light Grey (#E5E7E9).
Font Choices and Sizes
Typeface: Standard Sans-Serif (Helvetica or Arial).
Panel Labels (a-k): Bold, approx 12-14pt, aligned to the top-left of the subplot.
Axis Titles: Regular weight, approx 10pt.
Tick Labels: Regular weight, approx 8-9pt.
Bar Annotations: Vertical text centered on top of bars, regular weight, small size (approx 7-8pt).
Legend Text: Regular weight, small (8pt).
Line Weights and Marker Styles
Bar Charts:
Bars have no outline (linewidth = 0).
Baselines: Dashed black lines (linewidth = 1.0, linestyle='--').
Scatter Plots (Panel d):
Tiny markers (size 1-2), semi-transparent black/grey to show density.
Fit line: Solid thin black line.
Genomic Tracks (Panel b):
Filled area under the curve (alpha ~0.5) with a thin solid outline.
Swarm/Jitter Plot (Panel k):
Points: Small, varying opacity circles (o).
Error Bars: Thin grey lines behind the points.
Grid and Axis Styling
Spines: Classic "L-frame." Only Left and Bottom spines are visible. Top and Right spines are removed (despine).
Grid: None. No grid lines on any plot. The background is pure white.
Ticks: Outward facing, black, short length.
X-Axis Labels:
Panel f/j: Categorical ranges (e.g., "0-3 kb").
Panel k: Log-scale spacing labels.
Layout Proportions
Grid: A complex, irregular grid.
Row 1: Schematic (left) and wide genomic track (right).
Row 2 & 3: Small multiples of vertical bar charts (tall aspect ratio ~3:4).
Row 4: Wider charts (Stacked horizontal bars and grouped vertical bars).
Spacing: Consistent white gutters between panels.
Design Specifics
Bar Annotations: In the bar charts (c, e, f), the exact numerical value is often written vertically floating just above the bar.
Schematic Style: Use simple rectangles with no drop shadows for flowcharts. Arrows are thin, grey or black, with simple heads.
Sequence Logo (Panel b inset): Standard ATCG coloring (A=Green, T=Red, C=Blue, G=Yellow/Orange) with stacked letters scaling by height.
