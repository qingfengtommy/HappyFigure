Overall Aesthetic
The style is "High-Impact Academic" (Nature/Science style). It is characterized by a mixed-media layout (schematic diagrams combined with statistical plots), a clean white background, high-contrast text, and a complete absence of chartjunk (no grids, no background fills). The look is flat, precise, and minimalist.

Grid and Axis Styling
Spines: strict "L-frame" style. Only Left and Bottom spines are visible. Top and Right spines are removed.
Gridlines: None. The background is pure white.
Ticks: Outward facing, black, short length.
Axis Lines: Thin black lines (approx 0.8pt).
Layout Proportions
Top Half (Schematic): Irregular layout. Flowcharts and diagrams are arranged to guide the eye left-to-right.
Bottom Half (Data): Grid-based layout.
Row C: 3-column layout (Grouped Bar, Grouped Bar, Grouped Bar).
Row D: 5-column layout (Small Multiples of Bar Charts).
Row E: 4-column layout (Square Scatter Plots).
Whitespace: Generous spacing between panels; tight spacing within bar groups.
Design Between Subgraphs
Legend: A single shared legend for the quantitative plots (c, d) is placed horizontally between the diagram section and the data section.
Consistency: All bar charts share identical y-axis scaling where appropriate to allow comparison.
Alignment: The left edges of panels a, c, d, and e are vertically aligned.
